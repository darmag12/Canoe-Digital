Floor Plans

Here is an overview of the floor plans!

STUDIO C

Close proximity to The Galley and The Dock, the wraparound windows revealing Southern and Western views, and its ability to host photo shoots, business meetings, music videos, or parties make Studio C a favorite. Movable walls allow for expansion into Studio A and/or The Galley.

STUDIO A

Alone, Studio A features eighteen hundred square feet of uninterrupted views of the Hudson River and consistent light through it's west-facing windows. Movable walls allow for expansion into adjacent Studios C and/or N. The space is perfect for still-life shoots, castings, or business meetings.

STUDIO N

Need a large space with a cyclorama? By itself, Studio N features three thousand square feet of studio space and a cyclorama on the East wall. Movable walls allow for expansion into adjacent Studios A and/or O. The space is suitable for photo shoots with large sets, editorials, and commercials.

STUDIO O

Offering guests continuous Northern exposure, Studio O features a highly sought after amenity. In addition, the studio offers twenty-two hundred square feet of space and features a cyclorama on the East wall. Movable walls allow for expansion into adjacent Studios N and/or E. Studio O works well for fashion, beauty, on-figure catalog work, and editorial photo shoots.

STUDIO E

Enjoy twenty-three hundred square feet of studio space, a cyclorama, and abundant Northern exposure in Studio E. Movable walls allow for expansion into adjacent Studios O and/or Paddle. Studio E is preferred for fashion and catalog work as well as music videos and food shoots.

BELLA CORSA STUDIO

Introducing Canoe's newest Studio, The Bella Corsa Studio encompasses everything exclusive including a private entrance, private kitchen, and a private bathroom for our clients to enjoy. Bella Corsa has a cyclorama and viewing conference room. Totaling 3,600 sf this is Canoe's largest premium studio boasting complete exclusivity and premium Northern exposure and views. The ability to host photo shoots, business meetings, music videos, or parties makes Studio Bella Corsa a must for our high-end clients.
